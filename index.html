Edmonton Itinerary

4 Days / 3 Nights · Dec 11–14 · Public Transit Friendly · Detailed Activities & Helpful Links

Day 1 — Thu, Dec 11 — Arrival & Downtown Exploration

7:15 AM
  • Depart Vancouver (YVR) on your flight.
9:50 AM
  • Arrive at Edmonton International Airport (YEG). Airport Info
Take Route 747 Airport Shuttle Bus to Century Park LRT Station (approx. 30 min). Shuttle Info
10:45 AM
  • Take LRT from Century Park to Churchill Station downtown (approx. 25 min). LRT Map & Schedule
11:15 AM
  • Check in or drop off luggage at your downtown hotel.
If early check-in isn’t available, store your bags and explore nearby cafés.
12:00 PM
  • Lunch at Bundok — chef-driven seasonal shared plates restaurant.
1:30 PM
  • Choose one:
    Visit the Art Gallery of Alberta — contemporary art and rotating exhibits.
    OR
    Explore 104 Street (Warehouse District) — stroll historic buildings, boutiques, and artisan shops.
    Stop by Credo Coffee at 10134 104 St. for quality, affordable coffee in a cozy setting.
3:30 PM
  • Walk around Churchill Square and enjoy shops and street art.
5:30 PM
  • Dinner at La Taqueria — Known for authentic tacos and casual vibe.
7:30 PM
  • Evening stroll along the River Valley Trails — enjoy winter lights and crisp air.

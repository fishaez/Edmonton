Edmonton — 4 Days / 3 Nights Itinerary

December 11–14 — transit-friendly (ETS LRT & buses) · No driving required

Dates: Thu Dec 11 — Sun Dec 14
Mode: Public transport / walking

Day 1 — Thu, Dec 11 — Arrival & Downtown Lights

Afternoon — Arrival & Hotel
  • Arrive YEG → take Route 747 Airport Bus to Century Park LRT → LRT to Churchill Station. Check into downtown hotel.
  • 2:30 PM — Late lunch near hotel (Bundok or Dorinku Osaka).
Evening — Light Walk & Dinner
  • 4:30 PM — Downtown Winter Light Walk: Churchill Square → City Hall Plaza → Art Gallery of Alberta exterior lights.
  • 6:00 PM — Dinner: Rostizado (104 St) or Baijiu. Explore the Christmas market if running.
Transit notes: All downtown walking is short; LRT stops: Churchill, Bay/Enterprise Square.

Day 2 — Fri, Dec 12 — West Edmonton Mall & Indoor Fun

Morning
  • 8:30 AM — Breakfast at Little Brick Café (Riverdale). Bus #8 from downtown.
  • 10:00 AM — Bus 100 from downtown → West Edmonton Mall.
All day
  • 10:30 AM–4:00 PM — West Edmonton Mall: Galaxyland (rides), World Waterpark (swim optional), Ice Palace (skating).
  • Lunch inside the mall; lots of cafés and themed areas.
Evening
  • 4:30 PM — Bus 100 back downtown.
  • 6:00 PM — Dinner at Sabor; optional: Rogers Place for Oilers game or concert (check schedule).
Transit notes: Bus 100 is the direct frequent route. Consider a Day Pass for convenience.

Day 3 — Sat, Dec 13 — Two options (choose based on weather)

Plan A — Mild Cold (outdoor + lights)

Morning
  • 8:30 AM — Breakfast at Cafe Bicyclette (French Quarter). Bus #4 from downtown.
  • 10:00 AM — Muttart Conservatory (Bus #8 or #151). Warm tropical pyramids — great winter escape.
Afternoon
  • 12:00 PM — Lunch at The Next Act. 1:30 PM — Stroll Whyte Avenue (shops & cafés).
  • 3:00 PM — Warm up at a café (Block 1912 or Remedy).
Evening
  • 5:00 PM — Dinner at The Marc. 6:30 PM — Zoominescence at Edmonton Valley Zoo (Bus #14).
  • Return ~9:00 PM by bus.

Plan B — Extreme Cold (mostly indoors)

Morning
  • 8:30 AM — Cafe Bicyclette breakfast (same). 10:00 AM — TELUS World of Science (LRT to Kingsway/Royal Alex + Bus #125).
Afternoon
  • 12:15 PM — Kingsway Mall lunch & browse. 2:00 PM — Art Gallery of Alberta (LRT to Churchill).
Evening
  • 5:00 PM — Dinner at The Marc. 6:30 PM — Movie at Landmark Cinemas City Centre or Neon Sign Museum + hot drink.
Tip: Decide in the morning based on temperature & wind chill. Both plans use frequent ETS routes and short walks.

Day 4 — Sun, Dec 14 — Markets & Departure

Morning
  • 8:30 AM — Brunch at OEB Breakfast Co. 9:30 AM — Bus to Old Strathcona Farmers' Market (indoor).
  • 11:00 AM — Quick Whyte Ave stroll for souvenirs.
Afternoon — Departure
  • 12:00 PM — Light lunch. 1:30 PM — LRT to Century Park → Route 747 Airport Bus to YEG for departure.
Transit notes: allow buffer for luggage on buses; consider calling hotel for a late check-out if flight is late afternoon.

Quick practical tips

  • Buy an ETS Day Pass when you plan to make multiple trips in a day.
  • Dress in layers: thermal base, sweater, insulated down coat, winter boots, toque, gloves.
  • Daylight is short in December — aim to do outdoor activities earlier.
  • Check attraction opening hours & event dates (some festivals/markets change yearly).

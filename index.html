Edmonton — 4 Days / 3 Nights Detailed Transit-Friendly Itinerary

Dates: Thu Dec 11 – Sun Dec 14
Transport: ETS LRT & Bus (no driving)

Day 1 — Thu, Dec 11 — Arrival & Downtown Lights

12:00 PM
  • Arrive at YEG Airport → Route 747 Airport Bus to Century Park LRT → LRT to Churchill Station.
2:30 PM
  • Check into downtown hotel, drop bags.
  • Late lunch at Bundok (104 St) — modern Canadian dishes.
4:30 PM
  • Winter Light Walk: Churchill Square → City Hall Plaza → Art Gallery of Alberta exterior.
6:00 PM
  • Dinner at Rostizado (Mexican rotisserie) or Baijiu (Asian fusion).
7:30 PM
  • If open: Downtown Christmas Market at ATB Place.

Day 2 — Fri, Dec 12 — West Edmonton Mall Adventure

8:30 AM
  • Breakfast at Little Brick Café (Bus #8 from downtown).
10:00 AM
  • Bus #100 from downtown to West Edmonton Mall.
10:30 AM – 1:00 PM
  • Galaxyland: Indoor amusement park rides and games.
1:00 PM – 2:00 PM
  • Lunch inside the mall (Europa Boulevard café or Bourbon Street restaurants).
2:00 PM – 4:00 PM
  • World Waterpark: Tropical indoor pool and slides or Ice Palace skating rink.
4:30 PM
  • Bus #100 back downtown.
6:00 PM
  • Dinner at Sabor (Portuguese seafood).
7:00 PM
  • Optional: Oilers hockey game or concert at Rogers Place (check schedule).

Day 3 — Sat, Dec 13 — Weather-Based Plans

Plan A — Mild Cold (Outdoor + Lights)

8:30 AM
  • Breakfast at Cafe Bicyclette (Bus #4).
10:00 AM
  • Muttart Conservatory (Bus #8 or #151) — explore tropical and arid plant pyramids.
12:00 PM
  • Lunch at The Next Act (Whyte Ave).
1:30 PM – 3:00 PM
  • Whyte Avenue stroll: indie shops, murals, cozy cafés.
3:00 PM – 4:30 PM
  • Warm up at Block 1912 Café or Remedy Café.
5:00 PM
  • Dinner at The Marc (downtown).
6:30 PM – 9:00 PM
  • Zoominescence at Edmonton Valley Zoo (Bus #14) — illuminated animal sculptures.

Plan B — Extreme Cold (Mostly Indoors)

8:30 AM
  • Breakfast at Cafe Bicyclette.
10:00 AM – 12:15 PM
  • TELUS World of Science (LRT to Kingsway/Royal Alex + Bus #125).
12:15 PM – 1:45 PM
  • Lunch & browsing at Kingsway Mall.
2:00 PM – 4:00 PM
  • Art Gallery of Alberta (LRT to Churchill Station).
5:00 PM
  • Dinner at The Marc.
6:30 PM – 9:00 PM
  • Movie at Landmark Cinemas City Centre or Neon Sign Museum + hot drink.

Day 4 — Sun, Dec 14 — Markets & Departure

8:30 AM
  • Brunch at OEB Breakfast Co. (Rice Howard Way).
9:30 AM – 11:00 AM
  • Old Strathcona Farmers’ Market (Bus #8 or #701).
11:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  • Whyte Avenue souvenir shopping.
12:00 PM – 12:45 PM
  • Light lunch at Meuwly’s or Da Capo Café.
1:30 PM
  • LRT to Century Park → Route 747 Airport Bus to YEG for departure.

Tip: Buy an ETS Day Pass for unlimited daily travel. Dress warmly in layers, as Edmonton’s December weather can be extreme.

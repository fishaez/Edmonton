Edmonton Itinerary

4 Days / 3 Nights — Dec 11–14 · Transit Friendly · Includes Intimate Moments

Day 1 — Thu, Dec 11 — Arrival & Downtown Lights

7:15 AM
  • Depart Vancouver (YVR) on your flight.
9:50 AM
  • Arrive at Edmonton International Airport (YEG).
After arrival, collect luggage and take Route 747 Airport Shuttle Bus to Century Park LRT Station (approx. 30 min).
10:45 AM
  • Take LRT from Century Park to Churchill Station downtown (approx. 25 min).
11:15 AM
  • Check in or drop off luggage at your downtown hotel (early check-in may not be available).
If early check-in isn’t possible, consider storing your bags and grabbing brunch nearby.
12:00 PM
  • Lunch at Bundok or Dorinku Osaka — casual and close to downtown hotels.
1:30 PM
  • Find a cozy café or quiet spot downtown for a playful, intimate moment — a stolen kiss, gentle touch, or whisper to build anticipation.
Look for less busy indoor spots to stay discreet but connected.
2:30 PM
  • Winter Light Walk: Churchill Square → City Hall Plaza → Art Gallery of Alberta exterior.
Enjoy festive lights and take photos while the sun sets early in December.
5:30 PM
  • Dinner at Rostizado or Baijiu.
8:00 PM
  • Return to your hotel for an extended, passionate private session — perfect time for slow, sensual or wild exploration in the comfort and privacy of your room.
Set the mood with lighting, music, or anything that excites you both.

Day 2 — Fri, Dec 12 — West Edmonton Mall Adventure

8:30 AM
  • Breakfast at Little Brick Café (accessible via Bus #8).
A cozy café with excellent coffee and fresh pastries to start your day.
10:00 AM
  • Take Bus #100 to West Edmonton Mall.
West Edmonton Mall is North America’s largest shopping mall, offering much more than shopping.
12:30 PM
  • Find a quiet lounge or a secluded corner in the mall for a quick, playful intimate moment — a teasing touch or kiss to keep the spark alive.
Look for less crowded seating areas or cozy cafés.
1:00 PM – 2:00 PM
  • Lunch inside the mall — many options including food courts and sit-down restaurants.
Try some local favorites or international cuisines — something for everyone.
2:00 PM – 4:00 PM
  • Visit World Waterpark or skate at Ice Palace skating rink.
World Waterpark is one of the world’s largest indoor waterparks — bring swimwear if you want to swim! Alternatively, rent skates and enjoy the rink.
4:30 PM
  • Bus #100 back downtown.
6:00 PM
  • Dinner at Sabor (Spanish cuisine) or check for events at Rogers Place arena.
Sabor is well-loved for its tapas and cocktails. Rogers Place hosts concerts and sports — great if you want evening entertainment.
9:00 PM
  • Return to your hotel for a more playful and wild intimate session — experiment, explore fantasies, or just unwind with passionate connection.
Consider bath time, massage oils, or any fun accessories you brought along.

Day 3 — Sat, Dec 13 — Weather-Based Plans

Plan A — Mild Cold

8:30 AM
  • Breakfast at Cafe Bicyclette (Bus #4).
This charming café offers European-style breakfast and excellent coffee.
10:30 AM
  • Quiet intimate moment at Muttart Conservatory — find a peaceful corner in one of the glass pyramids for a gentle, romantic connection.
The lush tropical pyramid can set a dreamy, warm atmosphere.
12:00 PM
  • Lunch at The Next Act on Whyte Avenue.
A popular gastropub with great comfort food and local brews.
1:30 PM – 3:00 PM
  • Stroll along Whyte Avenue’s shops, cafés, and galleries.
Whyte Avenue is Edmonton’s vibrant arts and culture district, perfect for souvenir shopping or a coffee break.
3:30 PM
  • Playful public moment — a quick discreet touch or kiss in a cozy café or boutique on Whyte Avenue.
Keep it subtle but fun while soaking in the local vibe.
5:00 PM
  • Dinner at The Marc.
An upscale restaurant with French-inspired cuisine in a cozy setting. Reservations recommended.
6:30 PM – 9:00 PM
  • Zoominescence light festival at Edmonton Valley Zoo (Bus #14).
A magical winter light display featuring animal-shaped lanterns, perfect for families and photography lovers.
9:30 PM
  • Private intimate session back at your hotel — relax and reconnect with slow, sensual moments or try something new and exciting.

Plan B — Extreme Cold

8:30 AM
  • Breakfast at Cafe Bicyclette.
10:00 AM – 12:15 PM
  • Visit TELUS World of Science (LRT + Bus #125).
Interactive science exhibits, planetarium shows, and an IMAX theatre make this a great indoor activity. Admission approx. $20 CAD.
12:30 PM
  • Find a cozy café in Kingsway Mall for a quick playful intimate moment — a teasing kiss or gentle touch.
12:15 PM – 1:45 PM
  • Lunch and browsing at Kingsway Mall.
A relaxed shopping center with various eateries and stores.
2:00 PM – 4:00 PM
  • Art Gallery of Alberta (LRT).
Explore contemporary and historic art exhibitions. Admission around $10 CAD. Check for any special exhibits during your visit.
5:00 PM
  • Dinner at The Marc.
6:30 PM – 9:00 PM
  • Catch a movie at Landmark Cinemas or visit the Neon Sign Museum and nearby café for a cozy evening.
Neon Sign Museum offers a nostalgic glimpse into Edmonton’s past illuminated signs. Great if you want a relaxed evening indoors.
9:30 PM
  • Private intimate time back at the hotel — unwind with slow, sensual connection or playful exploration.

Day 4 — Sun, Dec 14 — Markets & Departure

8:00 AM
  • Early brunch at OEB Breakfast Co. to fuel up for your day.
9:30 AM
  • Cozy, discreet moment in a café or quiet corner at Old Strathcona Farmers’ Market (Bus #8 or #701) — a gentle kiss or touch to savor your last morning together.
9:00 AM – 10:30 AM
  • Visit Old Strathcona Farmers’ Market for local shopping.
10:30 AM – 11:30 AM
  • Souvenir shopping and café stop on Whyte Avenue.
11:30 AM – 12:15 PM
  • Light lunch at Meuwly’s or Da Capo Café.
12:30 PM
  • Return to your hotel, collect luggage, and check out.
1:00 PM
  • LRT to Century Park, then Route 747 Airport Shuttle Bus to Edmonton International Airport (YEG).
Allow around 1.5 hours for transit, check-in, and security to catch your flight.
6:10 PM
  • Depart Edmonton (YEG) for Vancouver (YVR).
7:05 PM
  • Arrive back at Vancouver (YVR).
